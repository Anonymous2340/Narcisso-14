What is Emoji?

Emoji are small images, symbols, or icons used in electronic communication. They are embedded in text and used to express objects, ideas, and emotions. Emoji are created using a drawing tool and are incorporated into a unique 16-bit Unicode to make them compatible with different devices and platforms. Emoji can include faces, people, animals, food, places, and more.
